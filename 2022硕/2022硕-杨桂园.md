论文总结：（Process intensification of hydrogen production by catalytic steam methane reforming: Performance analysis of multilayer perceptron- artificial neural networks and nonlinear response surface techniques）
* 问题的提出：
* 需要一个东西来解决输入参数与输出产量之前的关系，即研究输入参数的改变对输出的影响。输入：甲烷催化制氢输出:产量和转化率.
* 研究对象：（hydrogen generation by catalytic steam methane reforming）甲烷催化水蒸气重整制氢.
* 结构:
*   Training methods (scaled conjugate and gradient des-cent), hidden layer variation, artificial neuron variation, and activation functions.
* 原因：
* Besides improving the catalytic properties through various stra-tegies,  the  operating  conditions  of  the  steam  methane  reforming reactions can be optimized to improve the activity and stability of the  catalysts.  
*  The  use  of opti-mized process conditions can help to reduce catalyst deactivation by carbon deposition and hence improve its activity.
* 方法1：数学方法与人工智能神经网络  （Mathematical techniques and ANN）
* 输入：the input parameters which include flowrate of the reactor, the temperature of the reactor, steam to carbon ratio of the reactor, the flow rate of the furnace.
* 输出：outputs (temperature, velocity, pressure, and mole fraction of components).
*   predicted the temperature, velocity,  pressure,  and  mole  fraction of components  with  R2 of 0.9891. R平方为回归平方和与总离差平方和的比值，表示总离差平方和中可以由回归平方和解释的比.
* 方法2：a backpropagation feed-forward  artificial  neural  network（一种反向传播前馈人工神经网络）
* 方法3：multilayer percep-tron and radial basis function ANN（多层感知器和径向基）
*   the multilayer perceptron ANN has a better performance in predicting the hydrogen-rich syngas production compared to the radial basis function-ANN.
* A  Multi-Layer  Perceptron-Artificial  Neural Network (MLP-ANN) and non-linear response surface method are used to predict hydrogen generation by catalytic steam methane reforming.

* 本文方法：
*   A  Multi-Layer  Perceptron-Artificial  Neural Network (MLP-ANN) and non-linear response surface method.A data-driven approach，
* 模型建立：
* Step one：data generated （a steam methane reforming experiment）
* ：：input para-meters such as methane partial pressure, steam partial pressure, and the reaction temperature while the targeted output consists of hydrogen yield and methane conversion.
* Step two: (Model Configuration and optimization)模型配置与优化 
*   the usage of the appropriate number of artificial neurons in the hidden layer, how these artificial neurons are connected, and the proper algorithms that can be em-ployed for learning the process.
* 隐藏层，神经元，算法
* 理论假设：the layers of the artificial neurons are organized progressively, with each layer more closely compacted than the last: Hidden layers should always have the  same  activation  function  unless  they are  located on  the  same level; Neurons in the same layer do not communicate or interact with each other.
*      The ANN models in this study were configured by con-sidering the effects of activation functions and the number of layers. Hyperbolic tangent  and  sigmoid  functions  were  employed  at  the hidden and outer layers of each of the configurations.
*   评估手段：The per-formance of the various ANN models during training was evaluated based on the predicted errors which is measured as a function of the sum of squared error (SSE). While the predictability of the configured ANN was evaluated using MSE, MAPE and R2.

* Core：The inputs to the network include steam partial  pressure,  methane  partial  pressure  and  the  reaction  temperature, while the targeted variables are hydrogen yield and methane con-version. The dataset was trained using scaled conjugate and gradient descent algorithms with hyperbolic tangent and sigmoid activation function incorporated at the hidden and the output layer. The back-propagation approach was adopted to optimize the hidden neuron. The backpropagation algorithm work in such a way to minimize the predicted error by adjusting the network weight and bias. An opti-mized weight and bias often resulted in better predictions.
